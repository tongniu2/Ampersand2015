ABOUT ACIVITIES NIGHT


What is Activities Night? Every year the SSMU Clubs and Services team holds two Activities Night events - one at the beginning of the Fall semester and one at the beginning of the Winter semester - promote the hundreds of student groups on campus.

Activities Night is a hub for student engagement and campus involvement, with 300+ clubs, services, publications, faculty associations, and other student groups under one roof in the SSMU Building! Chat with the student executives running these groups and sign up to join the mailing list of the clubs you're interested in - many of the groups tabling at Activities Night are looking for new members and even new executive members!

This year, for the first time ever, we will be holding a 2-day Winter Activities Night which will run from 4pm - 8pm on Tuesday, January 12th and Wednesday, January 13th in the SSMU Building. Join us to learn all there is to know about getting involved at McGill!

FOR TABLING CLUBS SERVICES

Before the Event Checklist ! Have you done the following?
  • Sign your club up by filling out this form. Registration ends January 8th at noon! Once you sign up, you will receive confirmation emails by January 8th (if not earlier).
  • Pick up passes and pay for tables at the SSMU Front Desk. Pick up times are Friday, January 8th from 12 p.m. - 7 p.m. and Monday, January 11th from 12 p.m. - 7 p.m.
  • Distribute tabling passes to your members and make sure your club has a plan for who will be tabling when.

To ensure a smooth transition between tabling shifts, make sure your members know who will be tabling before and after them.

Missed Passes Pickup? Final pass pick up will be Tuesday morning, from 9AM - 11AM, at the SSMU Front Desk.

Hungry? This year, the Student Run Cafeteria will be open, so if you get hungry mid-tabling shift you can go and grab yourself a snack!

Too much stuff? You can now leave your promotional materials at your table overnight! We will be locking all the rooms. Please do not leave any valuables. We cannot guarantee the safety of the items you leave.
