ABOUT Ampersand:the Conference


What is Activities Night? Every year the SSMU Clubs and Services team holds two Activities Night events - one at the beginning of the Fall semester and one at the beginning of the Winter semester - promote the hundreds of student groups on campus.

Activities Night is a hub for student engagement and campus involvement, with 300+ clubs, services, publications, faculty associations, and other student groups under one roof in the SSMU Building! Chat with the student executives running these groups and sign up to join the mailing list of the clubs you're interested in - many of the groups tabling at Activities Night are looking for new members and even new executive members!

This year, for the first time ever, we will be holding a 2-day Winter Activities Night which will run from 4pm - 8pm on Tuesday, January 12th and Wednesday, January 13th in the SSMU Building. Join us to learn all there is to know about getting involved at McGill!
